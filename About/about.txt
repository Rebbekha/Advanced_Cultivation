[center][size=20pt]Advanced Cultivation[/size][/center]

[color=orange][size=18pt]Description:[/size][/color]
Advanced Cultivation aims to diversify the farming experience in Rimworld. To this aim, soil has been reworked and composting and fertilizer have been introduced!

[color=orange][b]Overwritten Vanilla Ground:[/b][/color]
[list]
[li]Soil
[list][li]Fertility: %70[/li]
[/list][/li][/list]
[list]
[li]Rich Soil
[list][li]Fertility: %100[/li]
[/list][/li][/list]
[list]
[li]Marshy Soil
[list][li]Fertility: %50[/li]
[li]Dries to: Rich Soil[/li]
[/list][/li][/list]
[list]
[li]Gravel
[list][li]Fertility: %45[/li]
[/list][/li][/list]
[list]
[li]Lichen-Covered Dirt
[list][li]Fertility: %70[/li]
[/list][/li][/list]
	
[color=orange][b]New Ground Types:[/b][/color]
[list]
[li]Tilled Soil 
[list]
[li]Has %90 fertility[/li]
[li]Requires the "Tilling" research[/li]
[li]Only takes work to make![/li]
[/list]
[/li][/list]
[list]

[/list][/li][/list]

[color=orange][b]New Items:[/b][/color]
[list]
[li]Raw Compost
[list][li]Requires the "Composting" research[/li]
[li]Costs .25 nutrition (~5 meat) per compost[/li]
[li]Can be crafted in batches of 5[/li]
[li]Craft at crafting spot or butcher table[/li]
[/list][/li][/list]
[list]
[li]Fermented Compost
[list][li]Raw Compost naturally ferments over time (10 days by default)[/li]
[li]Compost Bins allow compost to ferment faster (6 days by default)[/li]
[/list][/li][/list]
[list]
[li]Fertilizer
[list][li]Requires the "Simple Cultivation" research[/li]
[li]Costs 5 fermented compost per fertilizer[/li]
[li]Craft at a Butcher Table or Drug Lab[/li]
[/list][/li][/list]
[list]
[li]Enriched Fertilizer
[list][li]Requires the "Advanced Cultivation" research[/li]
[li]Costs 7 fermented compost per fertilizer[/li]
[li]Craft at a Drug Lab[/li]
[/list][/li][/list]
[list]
[li]Miracle Fertilizer
[list][li]Requires the "Miracle Cultivation" research[/li]
[li]Costs 10 fermented compost per fertilizer[/li]
[li]Craft at a Drug Lab[/li]
[/list][/li][/list]
If you don't like the increased difficulty from the soil changes, feel free to modify the Terrain_AC.xml.

[color=orange][b]Conflicts[/b][/color]
This mod alters vanilla ground objects (soil, rich soil, gravel, march, and lichen). Any mod loaded after it which alters these terrain will override these changes. Other than that, I don't anticipate any conflicts.
